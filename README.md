# 🌍 Project Atlas

> *Everything you need for your international education journey, in one place.*

## 📖 About

Project Atlas began with a simple observation.

Applying to study abroad is overwhelming.

Students often juggle university websites, scholarship portals, spreadsheets, emails, PDFs, YouTube videos, WhatsApp groups, and personal notes just to keep track of their applications. Important deadlines are missed, documents become scattered, and valuable opportunities are easily overlooked.

Project Atlas was born from this real experience.

It started as a personal project to solve one student's problem. The long-term vision is to evolve it into an intelligent platform that helps students navigate every stage of their international education journey—from discovering universities to securing funding, managing applications, preparing for departure, and building an international career.

---

## 🎯 Vision

To build the world's most student-centered platform for international education planning and opportunity discovery.

---

## 🚀 Mission

Simplify the study abroad journey by bringing planning, applications, scholarships, funding, documents, deadlines, and career preparation into one intelligent system.

---

## 📍 Current Status

**Phase:** Product Discovery

Project Atlas is currently in the research and planning stage.

During this phase, the focus is on understanding the problem deeply, documenting requirements, designing the product, and validating ideas before writing code.
