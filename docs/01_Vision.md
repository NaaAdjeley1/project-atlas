# Vision

## Vision Statement

To build the world's most student-centered platform for international education planning and opportunity discovery.

## What this means

Project Atlas aims to become the first place students turn to when planning their international education journey.

Instead of searching across dozens of websites, documents, emails, and spreadsheets, students should be able to access the information, tools, and guidance they need in one place.

The long-term vision is to simplify every stage of the journey—from discovering universities and scholarships to managing applications, securing funding, preparing for relocation, and building an international career.
